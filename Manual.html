Meet The Hive

The Hive Collection features five easygoing, office-friendly plants matched to you through a quick personality quiz. Each one is here to brighten your hive and help you grow—just what every busy bee needs.

Caring for your plant is simple. Start with the basics below.

Water

Water when the top of the soil feels dry. Keep it lightly moist—not soaked.

Note: Cactus prefers watering every 2–3 weeks. Pothos may need water more often, depending on light and soil.

Sun

Most Hive plants love bright, indirect light. Pothos can tolerate lower light, while cactus enjoys more direct sun.

Soil

Each plant is already potted in well-draining soil. No need to repot—just make sure it doesn’t stay soggy. Coleus and pothos like soil that holds gentle moisture, while cactus prefers it dry between waterings.
